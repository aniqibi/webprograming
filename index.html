About Our Society

Founded in 2004, Dewan Welfare Society has been at the forefront of social change, working tirelessly to uplift marginalized communities in Sagri village and surroundings. Our team of dedicated professionals and volunteers believe in creating sustainable solutions to poverty, inequality, and lack of access to basic services.

We operate with transparency and accountability, ensuring that every donation reaches those who need it most. Our programs are designed to empower individuals and communities to break the cycle of poverty and build a better future.
